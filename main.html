Ceramic Coating

Years of protection, not months. The ultimate shield for your vehicle's paint

Ceramic coatings chemically bond to your paint's finish, providing superior protection against UV rays, bird droppings, water spots, and harsh chemicals used by automatic car washes. Unlike waxes that last 1-3 months or polymer sealants lasting 4-6 months, ceramic coatings provide years of protection.

The coating adds an incredibly deep glossy shine with self-cleaning and healing effects, making it more difficult for dirt and contaminants to adhere to your vehicle. Washing and maintaining your vehicle becomes easy and enjoyable, while you stay better equipped to handle environmental challenges your vehicle faces every day.
